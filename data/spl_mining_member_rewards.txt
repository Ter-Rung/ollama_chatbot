The Member Rewards represents the points earned within your team. By inviting more individuals to join your team using your referral code, your member score escalates, enhancing your mining pace.

Member Rewards

Rewards = ( User type + Verified mining team * b)

Verified mining team: is a member who is referred by you and is in mining status and verified email.

User type:

Normal = 1
Pioneer = 3
Distributor = 5
Partner = 7

With: b = 25%
